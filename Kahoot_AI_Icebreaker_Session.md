# Kahoot Ice-Breaker: AI at Work
### Community Talk — Setup Guide

**Total questions:** 17 (3 polls + 2 word clouds + 8 quiz questions + 1 podium moment)
**Estimated time:** 20–25 minutes
**Tone:** Witty, inclusive, zero judgment

> **Kahoot setup tip:** Create one Kahoot game with all question types mixed in order below.
> Use the "Lobby music" feature and a fun title — suggested: **"Are You Smarter Than a Chatbot?"**

---

## HOW TO SET UP EACH TYPE IN KAHOOT

| Kahoot Type | When to Use | Points? |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| **Poll** | Opinion questions — no right/wrong | No |
| **Word Cloud** | Free-text, everyone types a word | No |
| **Quiz** | Multiple choice with a correct answer | Yes (speed matters) |
| **True or False** | Two options, one correct | Yes |

### QUIZ Q1 — The Classic
**Type:** Quiz (multiple choice)
**Time:** 20 seconds
**Points:** Standard
**Question:**
> When an AI confidently tells you something completely made up, that's called...?

| Option | Text | Correct? |
|--------|------|----------|
| A | A hallucination | ✅ YES |
| B | A creative interpretation | ❌ |
| C | Premium content | ❌ |
| D | A consultant's first draft | ❌ |

**Host tip after reveal:** *"Hallucination. Not a bug, not a lie — just AI doing its best impression of knowing things."*

---

### QUIZ Q2 — Prompting Reality Check
**Type:** Quiz (multiple choice)
**Time:** 25 seconds
**Points:** Standard
**Question:**
> You ask AI to "write a report." It writes a 10-page essay on the history of reports. What should you have done?

| Option | Text | Correct? |
|--------|------|----------|
| A | Been more specific in your prompt | ✅ YES |
| B | Blamed the intern | ❌ |
| C | Accept it, print it, submit it | ❌ |
| D | Switch back to Excel | ❌ |

**Host tip after reveal:** *"Garbage in, garbage out. The clearer your prompt, the better the output — this is literally the most valuable skill you can build right now."*

---

### QUIZ Q3 — True or False
**Type:** True or False
**Time:** 15 seconds
**Points:** Standard
**Question:**
> True or False: AI "understands" everything it tells you

| Option | Correct? |
|--------|----------|
| True | ❌ |
| False | ✅ YES |

**Host tip after reveal:** *"LLMs predict what text should come next based on patterns — they don't 'understand' finance, law, or life. They generate plausible-sounding text. Your expertise is what validates it."*

### QUIZ Q4 — Agent Edition

|--------|------|----------|

### QUIZ Q7 — Token Economy
**Type:** Quiz (multiple choice)
**Time:** 20 seconds
**Points:** Standard
**Question:**
> What is a "token" in AI? (Not the crypto kind)

| Option | Text | Correct? |
|--------|------|----------|
| A | A unit of text AI processes — roughly a word | ✅ YES |
| B | The currency you pay for ChatGPT | ❌ |
| C | An access badge for the AI data center | ❌ |
| D | A loyalty reward from your AI subscription | ❌ |

**Host tip after reveal:** *"Tokens are how AI services measure and charge usage — input and output both cost tokens. Knowing this helps you understand why pasting a 200-page document into a chat might get expensive fast."*

---

### QUIZ Q8 — The Grand Finale
**Type:** Quiz (multiple choice)
**Time:** 30 seconds
**Points:** Double points
**Question:**
> AI drafts a budget summary for your area — but the OpEx figure is €50K off. What's the most likely cause?

| Option | Text | Correct? |
|--------|------|----------|
| A | The context window was too small to process the full report | ❌ |
| B | The model hallucinated — it generated a plausible-sounding number, not a fact-checked one | ✅ YES |
| C | The prompt wasn't specific enough | ❌ |
| D | The model needs to be fine-tuned on your financial data | ❌ |

**Host tip after reveal:** *"A, C, and D are all things you might reasonably try to fix — but the root cause here is hallucination. LLMs generate text that sounds right; they don't verify numbers. The lesson: never trust an AI-produced figure without checking it against the source. Confidence is not accuracy."*
